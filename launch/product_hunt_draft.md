## Maker Comment / Story

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm Kyle, and I built A2Apex because of a gap I couldn't ignore.

Google's A2A protocol is about to become the standard for how AI agents talk to each other. But here's the thing: Google will never build a tool that tells developers "your agent FAILS compliance." That's terrible marketing for their own protocol.

So I built it.

**A2Apex does three things:**

1. **Tests** your agent against the full A2A spec
2. **Certifies** passing agents with Bronze/Silver/Gold badges
3. **Lists** certified agents in a public trust registry

Why does this matter? The agent economy is projected to hit $236B. When your agent needs to talk to other agents, trust isn't optional—it's infrastructure. Our badges work like SSL certs for the agent world: embed them in your README, and other developers know your agent actually speaks A2A.

**What we're NOT:**

We're not a debugging tool. We're not "A2A for beginners." We're the certification authority for agents that are ready to ship.

**Pricing:**
- Free: 3 tests/month (perfect for hobby projects)
- Pro ($29/mo): Unlimited testing for serious builders
- Enterprise ($499/mo): Permanent badges + dedicated support

Built this in a week with my AI co-builder. Sometimes the best tools come from seeing a gap and just... filling it.

Try it free at a2apex.io 🚀

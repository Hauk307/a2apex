## Production Notes

### Screen Recording Tips
- Use 1920x1080 or 1080x1080 (square for social)
- Clean browser (no bookmarks bar, no tabs)
- Incognito mode or dedicated demo browser profile
- Smooth, deliberate cursor movements (not too fast)

### Text Overlay Style
- Font: Clean sans-serif (Inter, SF Pro, or similar)
- Color: White with subtle drop shadow OR black on light backgrounds
- Position: Lower third or center
- Animation: Simple fade in/out (no spinning or bouncing)

### Timing Flexibility
- Can cut the Registry section (0:38-0:48) if running long
- Can extend the Test Running section with more dramatic pauses
- Hook and CTA are non-negotiable—nail those

### Music Suggestions (royalty-free)
- Epidemic Sound: Search "tech" or "innovation"
- YouTube Audio Library: "Bright" or "Inspiring" categories
- Or skip music entirely—silent with text can be powerful
